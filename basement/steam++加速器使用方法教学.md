# Steam++加速器使用方法
            
## github由于是国外网站我们常常需要加速器方可访问

### 使用方法
>1.点击网址[https://gitee.com/rmbgame/SteamTools/releases/tag/3.0.0-rc.7](https://gitee.com/rmbgame/SteamTools/releases/tag/3.0.0-rc.7)
 
- 跳转后如图所示
 ![alt text](all_image/steamPP_image/image0.png) 

> 2.点击箭头下载

 ![alt text](all_image/steamPP_image/image1.png)

> 3.点击安装包安装

![alt text](all_image/steamPP_image/image2.png)

> 4.安装并自定义安装至其他盘（我选了D:）

![alt text](all_image/steamPP_image/image3.png)

![alt text](all_image/steamPP_image/image4.png)

![alt text](all_image/steamPP_image/image5.png)

![alt text](all_image/steamPP_image/image6.png)

> 5.进入起始页
![alt text](all_image/steamPP_image/image7.png)

> 6.点击网络加速
![alt text](all_image/steamPP_image/image8.png)

>7.选择github加速
![alt text](all_image/steamPP_image/image9.png)

> 8.点击一键加速即可
![alt text](all_image/steamPP_image/image10.png)

>最后,便可稳定访问github了
